Gladly Account Scoring Engine

Gladly scores inbound accounts using a two-part model - Fit from ZoomInfo and Clearbit, Timing from 6sense and first-party signals. Clay normalizes the record, Claude reasons over it, Salesforce stores it, and Marketo fires the right motion. Every slider on this page feeds the same engine.
