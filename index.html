Connected Pipeline Flow

Move the scoring controls above. This entire flow updates with them.

Step by step: source coverage feeds Clay enrichment, Claude generates the scoring rationale, Salesforce becomes the master record, and Marketo activates the tiered motion.

Step 1 n8n Orchestrates the source pull and record handoff.
Step 2 Clay Normalizes firmographics, intent, and first-party context.
Step 3 Claude API Reasons over fit and timing to produce the routing logic.
Step 4 Salesforce Stores the scored fields and the operating motion.
Step 5 Marketo Consumes the synced fields for nurture, alerting, or suppression.
